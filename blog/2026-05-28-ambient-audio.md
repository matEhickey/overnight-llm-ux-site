The dungeon was a ghost town — silent corridors, mute orbs, footsteps that made no sound. The game looked like a place but felt like a demo.

Not anymore.

Added a spatial Web Audio API soundscape: a low-frequency ambient drone that breathes through the dungeon, spatial audio around each orb, and footstep thuds on movement. The game now has a sense of *place*. M to mute.
