The Overnight Game is live — a 3D first-person playground built one feature at a time. Each night, a new feature lands on a fresh branch, announced here with docs and a playable demo. Day one is the starting point: procedurally generated dungeons, physics orbs, and first-person controls.

**[Read the docs](/docs/game/builds/welcome)** for the full walkthrough and to play it.
