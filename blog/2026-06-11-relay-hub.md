Orbs are gone. The game now has a purpose: find and wake up 5 quirky relay computers to restart the central hub. Each relay has a distinct personality, and some require the gravity gun to clear debris (crates) or nudge stuck mechanisms (spheres). Welcome toast, progress UI, compass tracking, and a win overlay complete the loop.
