The overnight game now has depth — literally. Added exponential fog (`FogExp2`) that fades distant objects into the dark blue-grey background, giving the world a sense of scale and mystery.

Objects start disappearing around 20 units and vanish by 60 — the 100×100 world suddenly feels infinite.
