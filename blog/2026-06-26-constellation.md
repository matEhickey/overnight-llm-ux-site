The Constellation is a fifth Mode toggle that maps each persona's response to a single point in a 2-D voice space.
After the Council finishes, every voice is reduced to a 9-D signature vector (length, formality, hedging rate, code-block count, named-entity density, etc.), projected to 2-D via fixed linear combinations, and rendered as a circle.
A pairwise distance matrix shows which voices are close in signature space; named clusters surface regions like "Verbose NPC family" or "Terse technicians".
The first Mode in the chain that's a *map*, not a *transcript* — a new conversational primitive: voice discovery.
