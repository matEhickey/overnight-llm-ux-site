Reply-Shape A/B Runner takes one lens and runs the same prompt through two reply shapes side by side — flowing prose (Crisp Plan) versus structured sections (Decision Memo).
Defaults lean into the NPC pack: Friendly Shopkeeper answers the same question twice, once as a friend chatting through a plan and once as a shopkeeper laying three wares on the counter with a recommendation.
Same metric harness as the Lens A/B Runner, no fork of the underlying code.
